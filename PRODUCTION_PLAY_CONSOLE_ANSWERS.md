Production Access Questionnaire — draft answers (based on tester report)

1) How did you recruit users for your closed test?
I engaged users through targeted online communities and social media platforms to ensure a diverse group of testers.

2) How easy was it to recruit testers for your app?
Easy

3) Describe the engagement you received from testers during your closed test
Testers provided valuable feedback on usability, feature enhancements, and overall app performance. Their active participation highlighted the need for better onboarding and customization options.

4) Provide a summary of the feedback that you received from testers.
Feedback highlighted the need for ASO optimization, dynamic walkthroughs, expanded language support, and a dark mode feature. We collected input through surveys, direct communication, and user interaction.

5) Who is the intended audience for your app?
Users seeking streamlined account management and convenience via Google registration. Tech-savvy users who want quick local price comparisons.

6) Describe how your app provides value to the users.
Esnaftaucuz simplifies finding the best local prices with community-shared, photo-backed price reports and location-based sorting.

7) How many installs do you expect your app to have in your first year?
10k - 100k

8) What changes did you make based on closed test feedback?
Implemented a dynamic onboarding walkthrough, improved ASO copy, and added dark mode and language selector scaffolding.

9) How did you decide that your app is ready for production?
After thorough testing and implementing key UX improvements from tester feedback, we validated stability across devices and fixed critical flows.
